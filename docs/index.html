Hello, I'm Eddy

I am a driven university student majoring in Computational Design, with interdisciplinary experience across the built environment, information technology, and digital content creation.

I am passionate about integrating innovation and creativity into every aspect of my work, from architectural design and 3D modeling to coding and content production. With a strong foundation in interior and exterior architecture, construction, landscape, and urban planning, I thrive on applying cutting-edge tools and technology, including Python, Grasshopper, HTML, and CSS, to solve complex design challenges when using 3D modelling software such as Rhino3D and Revit.

Additionally, I have hands-on experience in IT support, along with a background in videography, photography, and media creation, making me a versatile contributor to both technical and creative projects. My goal is to continuously grow in my career, embracing opportunities that allow me to innovate and make impactful contributions in design and technology.
